Crystal Collector

Instructions on how to play:


  • You will be assigned a random number at the start of the game.
  • By clicking a crystal below, you will add a random amount to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • Each time you win or lose, all scores are reset and the crystal values are randomized again.
